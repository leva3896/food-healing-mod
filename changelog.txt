Food Healing MOD - Changelog
=============================

v2.2.1 (2026-04-11)
-------------------
- Fixed: Food diversity tooltips no longer randomly disappear in JEI/Creative menus ("unstable" display bug fixed).
- Fixed: Added safety sync for food diversity on EntityJoinLevel to prevent synchronization misses during immediate logins.

v1.5.4 (2026-02-06)
-------------------
- Fixed: HP display now shifts up when armor is equipped to avoid overlapping with armor bar

v1.5.3 (2026-02-06)
-------------------
- Fixed: FoodHealingHandler now properly applies HP recovery and bonus effects
- Changed: Enabled FoodHealingHandler, disabled HungerChangeHandler to avoid duplication

v1.5.2 (Previous)
-----------------
- Initial features:
  - Food-based healing (nutrition x2 HP)
  - Bonus effects (Resistance IV + Fire Resistance) for high nutrition food
  - Always eat regardless of hunger level
  - Food diversity bonus (5 unique foods = +2 max HP)
  - Numerical HP display overlay
  - Configurable settings via foodhealing-common.toml
